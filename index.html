About Me

Deepayan Pal is a dynamic entrepreneur, mentor, and career transformation coach, passionate about shaping individuals into confident, future-ready professionals. As the Co-founder of Sikharthy Infotech Pvt. Ltd., he has played a pivotal role in building innovative solutions that bridge the gap between technology, learning, and career development. With a strong background in leadership and business growth, Deepayan combines his entrepreneurial expertise with a people-first approach, helping students, professionals, and startups discover their true potential. His vision is rooted in empowering individuals to turn challenges into opportunities, enabling them to thrive in competitive environments.

Beyond entrepreneurship, Deepayan is widely recognized for his impactful skill development workshops, career guidance sessions, and one-on-one mentoring. He has guided countless learners in identifying their strengths, enhancing their skills, and choosing the right career path. With a mission to inspire and equip the next generation of leaders, he blends practical insights with proven strategies to ensure long-term growth and success. His work at Sikharthy Infotech Pvt. Ltd. reflects his commitment to innovation and transformation, while his mentoring initiatives showcase his dedication to personal growth and career empowerment. For Deepayan, every journey is unique—and his purpose is to guide each one with clarity and purpose.
